# E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard: Ferns N Petals (FNP)

## 📌 Project Overview
This Data Analytics project transforms raw transactional data from Ferns N Petals (FNP) into structured, actionable business intelligence. The resulting interactive dashboard supports strategic decision-making by tracking revenue generation, operational logistics, and customer behavior.

## 💡 Key Analysis & Insights

*   **Revenue Generation:** Evaluated **₹35.2L** in total revenue across **1,000 completed orders**.
*   **Customer Valuation:** Identified an average customer transaction value of **₹3,520.98**.
*   **Operational Logistics:** Analyzed supply chain efficiency, revealing a mean order-to-delivery timeline of **5.53 days**.
*   **Granular Performance Breakdowns:** Visualized revenue distribution across several critical business dimensions:
    *   **Seasonal Occasions:** Isolated high-performing periods like Anniversaries and Raksha Bandhan.
    *   **Product Categories:** Identified *Sweets* and *Soft Toys* as top revenue drivers.
    *   **Temporal Patterns:** Analyzed sales velocity by Day of the Week and Month-over-Month revenue trends.
    *   **Geographic Distribution:** Tracked order volumes across the Top 10 Cities.

## 🛠️ Features & Technical Implementation

*   **Interactive Functionality:** Integrated dynamic slicers (`Order Date`, `Delivery Date`, and `Occasion`) allowing stakeholders to filter datasets and isolate specific trends seamlessly.
*   **Design Execution & Visual Hygiene:**
    *   Unified the color palette around the organization's green corporate identity.
    *   Eliminated distracting background gridlines to maintain a clean interface.
    *   Strategically aligned all charts and KPI cards to maximize data density and readability.
